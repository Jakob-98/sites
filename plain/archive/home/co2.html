The map gives an overview of CO2 emissions per capita for all countries engaging in the European Trading System (ETS).
Select a country in the map to see the emissions details of the country. Choose an ETS category to see distribution between the sectors covered in the ETS.
All data is in tonne CO2 equivalent. All greenhouse gasses under ETS are converted to their equivalent in CO2 with regard to greenhouse effect potency.
